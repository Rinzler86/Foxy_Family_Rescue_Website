About Our Rescue

Hi, I'm Taylor. I am the President of Foxy Family Rescue and Vice President of Exotic Pet Wonderland. For more information on our exotic rescue, please click here.

Foxy Family Rescue began in 2020 as a small scale rescue and has grown exponentially. We are currently in the process of applying to be a licensed non profit organization so we can continue to grow and help more animals in need.

We have provided a permanent home for countless dogs, cats and small pocket pets. We receive dozens of phone calls each week about abandoned or unwanted dogs and cats - and it's not slowing down any time soon.

Some stay here. Many are adopted out. You can see more information about adoptable pets by clicking on our pages above.
There is a small adoption fee associated with each animal. The amount will be listed on their individual pages. This adoption fee is required and helps cover the costs of rescue so we can continue to save as many dogs, cats and small pets in need as possible

Your new furry family member will be up to date on all required vaccinations before going home with you. In an effort to help the fight against overpopulation, all pets will also be spayed or neutered. There are no exceptions.

Our passion is our animals. We spend a large amount of time consulting with potential new pet parents to help determine which of our adoptable animals will be the best fit for them and their families. If you would like more information about our rescue or an available animal, please contact us.

Thank you,
Taylor Morgan
